Grand

Floral

"The honor of your presence is requested at the union of high quality flowers and superior design technique."

Grand Floral has served the community for 6 years offering fresh, high quality floral arrangements, plants and gifts for your events. We have a wide selection of flowers shipped in daily from several regions around the world. Grand Floral delivers locally in New York. Flowers are a powerful way to communicate important messages to your clients, family, friends, and employees. We give creative ideas, offer professional designs and give an unconditional guarantee of satisfaction. Explore the use of flowers for employee recognitions, business gifts; lobby, reception, or holiday decorations; conventions, corporate events, parties and marketing.

Grand Floral has been a big part in the community and has been a major contributor for the September 11th memorial. We are focused to helping non-profit organizations as well as professional business events. We like to provide to the needs of the community as much as possible. If there is something one of our clients wants and if we don't have it we will get it for them. Our clients are our reason for existing and we will do what it takes to make them happy. We appreciate your business and thank you for taking the time to learn more about us.
